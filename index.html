Living a SINGLE Life  

The Single life of an individual is explained in so many distinct ways. I have heard the word used as only one, sole, solitary, unaccompanied, and unattached. Some people may define single as being alone, lonely or maybe even a third wheel. Some may say it depend on how you look at it and others may say that being single can also be a blessing, but what does God say... (1Cor 7:7-9)

  Being made Whole

The word Whole can be interpreted as all of, entire, complete, full, intact, and unbroken. I think of the word whole as being complete in God and lacking nothing (Col 2:10). His word let us know that He is our provision, He is our protection, He is our deliverer, He is our healer, He is our refuge, He is our sustainer, He is our waymaker. God is whatever you need Him to be in your life. What do you need Him to be for you today?
